Organized by Ang Zhang and Ashley Teamer from Painting, GRIP was a dance music panel and performance featuring DJs and artists exploring the intersection of music, community, and visual art. Performing DJs Russell E.L. Butler, SCRAAATCH, and Shyboi are artists whose practices are at the intersection of party, performance, and sound.

For the event, I collaborated with fellow designers Daedalus Li and Sarah Elawad to produce a perforated poster and risograph flyers that were put up around campus, as well as a gif for social media. The way we approached this collective process was for each of us to first make individual designs for the event, and then we collaged all of our initial compositions into a final, mishmashed one. The poster consisted of many smaller, tiled poster-flyers that viewers could tear off and take with them.
