Runic typeface

Robofont
2022

First designed in 1901, Runic is a typeface that has been revived numerous times by foundries such as Nebiolo and Stephenson Blake. Using these many sources as reference, I revived Runic again for use as a body font, adding various characters not included in previous revivals.

My revival of Runic is the typeface used throughout this website.
